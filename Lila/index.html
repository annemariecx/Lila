LILA - a mindful resource for children

LILA is a web application that curates mindfulness themed content for children. Its simple, hassle-free interface allows children, educators and parents to find quality resources to encourage mindfulness in children. In Addition to that LILA aims to give children a sense of what keeps them healthy.


Please keep in mind that this platform does not replace the consultation with a professional psycologist or physician for mental or physical illnesses.
