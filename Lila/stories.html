Stories

Just like there is a world outside of us that we can explore, there also exists a rich and vibrant inner world inside us.
Here are stories to give you a sneak peek into the beautiful world that lives inside you!

  • What if you were to meet your anger one day? What would you do? Read what Anh does when he meets his anger for the first time.
    • 'Anh's Anger' by Gail Silver

  • The body talks to you. Do you know its language? Come, learn how to make your body your friend.
    • 'Listening to my Body' by Gaby Garcia.

  • Little Esperanza shows us that to be kind and loving towards others, you have to start from being kind and loving to yourself.
    • 'Listening with my Heart' by Gaby Garcia.

  • Each rock tells you a story about the Earth, about the beings who have lived on it. Each rock is unique, individual, just like you. Curious about how to make friends with a rock?
    • 'Everybody needs a Rock' by Byrd Baylor
